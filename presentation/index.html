One brain. Every surface.

Soul AI is the shared intelligence layer — not re-implemented on each device. Mobile, cockpit, and simulator are clients. The intelligence is shared.

Three product pillars.

Each surface contributes a distinct capability. Together they compound.

✈
Mobile

Personal Flight Concierge

Calendar-aware daily planning. Fly Plan generation. Fly-vs-drive decisions. Proactive briefing — before you ask.

  • Daily agenda + departure recommendation
  • Remote cabin preparation
  • Route feasibility & weather awareness
⬡
Cockpit / NavApp

Cockpit Command Companion

Voice commands validated against risk levels before any MQTT message is sent. Concise during critical flight phases.

  • Climate, lighting, navigation control
  • Real-time transcript & animation overlay
  • Safety-first intent validation
◎
Simulator + Telemetry

Flight Intelligence & Coaching

Ingests telemetry from MSFS, X-Plane, and Embention. Builds a persistent pilot profile over time. Coaches on real gaps.

  • Smoothness & stability scoring
  • Post-session debrief generation
  • Personalised training recommendations
